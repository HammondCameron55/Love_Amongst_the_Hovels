About the Recording

This audiobook constitutes the complete and unabridged audio recording of Love Amongst the Hovels — a work described by its own authors as "entirely historically accurate" with a degree of confidence that historians find either inspiring or alarming, depending on their disposition.


The recording captures all the psychological tension, the preachiness, and the love — yes, love, nestled improbably among the hovels — exactly as written. No historical events were harmed in the making of this audiobook, though several were gently bent into more convenient shapes.

📚 Genre

Historical Fiction · Satirical Comedy · Psychological Thriller
(Emphasis on the comedy. And the punishment.)

🗺️ Setting

Russia, early 1900s. A time of great mustaches, modest hovels, and an extraordinary quantity of existential brooding.

✍️ Authors

Eden Lyman & Emily Lyman — a literary duo united by shared surnames and a mutual appreciation for things going subtly, inexorably wrong.
